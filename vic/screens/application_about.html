APPLY TO PARTICIPATE.


As a wireless leader and innovator, Verizon is in a unique position to encourage and enable innovation among a broad group of business es, from start-ups to global players. With core expertise in wireless technology, Verizon is a natural partner for innovation. We know the hurdles and challenges, and can help you get through them. Here within the Verizon Innovation Program, we are focused on helping deliver the nextgeneration of products that will help make the Internet of Things a reality; projects target nontraditional wireless solutions in healthcare and retail, transportation, entertainment and beyond.

The following Verizon Innovation Program application involves answering targeted (but highlevel) questions about the company and proposed project —very likely questions that have already been asked in building your company or in attempting to build and sell your products. A team of engineers and analysts within the Verizon Innovation Program will use the information provided to gain a directional understanding of your project—and to extend select invitations for the next step in the evaluation process.

The Verizon Innovation Program accepts new participants on a rolling basis—you should apply now so that we can start to take action!


About the Application

The application to the Verizon Innovation Program involves four short-sections, should take approximately 15-20 minutes, and must be completed in one session:

  • Before You Apply (i.e., things to consider before applying)
  • Section 1: Tell Us About Your Company (i.e., basic info about your organization)
  • Section 2: Tell Us About Your Idea (i.e., pitch idea in several sentences, how you plan to utilize wireless, where help is needed)
  • Section 3: Tell Us About the Market Opportunity (i.e., target customers, alliances, size of opportunity)
  • Section 4: Tell Us How to Contact You (i.e., point of contact, if applicable -Salesforce.com ID)

Please note that all information you provide as part of the application process will be on a non-confidential basis.
