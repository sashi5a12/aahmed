Verizon Innovation Program Application

The following Verizon Innovation Program application involves answering targeted (but high-level) questions about the company and proposed project—very likely questions that have already been asked in building your company or in attempting to build and sell your products. A team of engineers and analysts within the Verizon Innovation Program will use the information provided to gain a directional understanding of your project—and to extend select invitations for the next step in the evaluation process.

About the Application

While accepted engagements within the Verizon Innovation Program are protected by Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA), all information you provide as part of the application process will be on a non-confidential basis.

Non-Confidentiality of Your Program Application.

Your Verizon Innovation Program Application (the "Application") does not create a confidential, fiduciary, or other type of special relationship with Verizon or its affiliates, and your Application, and anything else submitted by you hereunder or in related communications (such as any questions, comments, answers, correspondence, and the like) will be treated by Verizon as non-confidential and non-proprietary – regardless of whether you mark them "confidential", "proprietary", or the like. You agree that (a) your Application (and all related communications) will be treated as non-confidential and non-proprietary and will not be returned, and (b) Verizon and its affiliates do not assume any obligation of any kind to you or any third party with respect to the content or information contained in your Application or related communications. You hereby grant, and you agree to grant, to Verizon the worldwide, irrevocable, perpetual, and cost-free right and license to use any ideas, concepts, know-how, or techniques contained in your Application and related communications for any purposes whatsoever. You represent and warrant that the information you submit in connection with the Application does not contain the confidential or proprietary information of any third party.
